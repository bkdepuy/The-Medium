* I. Ontological Primitives
These symbols represent the irreducible "DNA" of the SFP. They move the framework beyond materialist "LEGO" blocks into a pure state-based logic.

1. **Intrinsic Distinction ($\iota$):** The eternal, irreducible "spark" or difference that precedes all structure[cite: 5, 24]. Status: Axiomatic.
2. **Activation ($V$):** The activation of $\iota$. This is not a set of values, but the "On" state of the potentiality. (Consequence of Axiom II).
3. **Eternal Medium ($\mathcal{M}$):** The foundational field with no beginning, end, or cause[cite: 7, 25]. Status: Axiomatic.

* II. The Mechanics of Locality
In SFP, "Space" is not a pre-existing container but a produced effect of the Medium's refresh rate[cite: 6, 10].

** 1. Locality Emergence (Axiom III)
Location is not fundamental; it is produced at the propagation limit $c$[cite: 12, 34].
- **Post-Locality Approximation ($M$):** The "Medium" as we perceive it after locality has been established[cite: 30].
- **Location ($x \in M$):** A specific coordinate produced only upon structure formation[cite: 33].

** 2. Population and Manifestation
The "Full Void" is populated by functions that determine what becomes "real" or "solid"[cite: 41, 64].
- **Population Function ($\phi : M \to V$):** A partial function mapping the Medium to its Activation state.
- **Manifestation Probability ($p(x)$):** The likelihood of a "Knot" or structure appearing at coordinate $x$[cite: 40].

* III. Dynamics and Constraints
Existence is a sequence of state transitions governed by the system's inherent latency[cite: 12, 55].

** 1. The Transition Index ($t$)
Time is redefined as a **Transition Index**—a counter for state changes rather than a flowing dimension[cite: 10].
- **State Transition:** $M_t \to M_{t+1}$

** 2. The Propagation Constraint
The Medium governs the rate at which "Potential" can shift between coordinates[cite: 36].
- **Standard Propagation:** #+BEGIN_SRC latex
  $$|\phi_{t+1}(x) - \phi_t(x)| \leq c$$
  #+END_SRC
  (The change in state cannot exceed the refresh rate $c$[cite: 34].)

- **Structure Formation (The Knot):**
  #+BEGIN_SRC latex
  $$|\phi_{t+1}(x) - \phi_t(x)| = c$$
  #+END_SRC
  When the state change hits the maximum limit ($c$), a **Tension Gradient** or "Locality" is formed[cite: 45, 50].

*** Falsification Anchor
If state changes are observed to exceed $c$ without the formation of a localized structure (Knot), the propagation constraint is invalidated[cite: 60].
